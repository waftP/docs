Get set up and create your first **payment** in Sandbox.

## Step 1: Set up your Waftpay environment

<AccordionGroup>
    <Accordion icon="key" title="Create Sandbox credentials">
        1. Open the **Waftpay Dashboard** and create a **Client ID** and **Client Secret** for Sandbox.
        2. Keep secrets **server-side only** (never expose in frontend or mobile apps).
        <Tip>Use different credentials for Sandbox and Production.</Tip>
    </Accordion>

    <Accordion icon="globe" title="Pick your base URL">
        - **Sandbox:** `https://sandbox.waftpay.io/api`
        - **Production:** `https://api.waftpay.io`

        Configure your app per environment to avoid mix-ups.
    </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Step 2: Get an access token

Exchange your client credentials for a short-lived **Bearer** token.

**cURL**
```bash
curl -X POST https://sandbox.waftpay.io/api/oauth/token \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{"grant_type":"client_credentials","client_id":"YOUR_CLIENT_ID","client_secret":"YOUR_CLIENT_SECRET"}'
