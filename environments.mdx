Waftpay provides **Sandbox** for testing and **Production** for live traffic.

## Base URLs

- **Sandbox:** `https://sandbox.waftpay.io/api`
- **Production:** `https://api.waftpay.io`

## Tips
- Use **separate** Client IDs/Secrets for each environment.
- Keep secrets on the **server** only.
- Point Sandbox webhooks to a **dev/staging** endpoint first.
- Log the environment alongside your request/response for easier debugging.
